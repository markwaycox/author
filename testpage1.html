[The weight-loss/weight-gain debate] 

When talking about Wellbeing, imagine the form of a Venn Diagram.

By separating the physical from the mental, from the social and from the spiritual we can more accurately break down a problem, then better channel our energies in finding an appropriate solution.

In my approach to Wellbeing, solutions can lean toward the scientific or the non-scientific as well, while not closing the door on certain combinations of the two. 

Also, due to the complex health system at hand, solutions need to contain reason.

Here's an example of this technique using the physical realm:

Statistics are clearly showing that many individuals are carrying too much weight. My experience as a personal trainer, yoga instructor, ESL teacher of kids, and simply people watching (a longstanding hobby favorite of mine) backs up many of these gruesome stats. 

I don't at all hold a one-size vision of society. But what is obvious is the ineffectiveness of all that has been tried up to now; the problem slogs on. 

So, as yo-yo dieting remains constant, as people continue to suffer from chronic pain, illness and disease, the questions begs: "How can we connect to a comprehensive solution?" 

The solution must be:                  

1.     highly accessible

2.     affordable to all

3.     efficient and successful
